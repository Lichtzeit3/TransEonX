YoRHa No. 2 Type B, also known as 2B, from NieR:Automata has now joined the PLAY ARTS KAI action figure line!
The textures on 2B's hair and clothing exude a sense of luxury. Every fine detail and proportion has been recreated carefully and faithfully when crafting this masterpiece.

This figure comes with the accessories: the beautifully made Virtuous Contract weapon, along with plenty of exchangeable hand parts as you get with the Standard Ver.

In addition to those, the glorious Deluxe Ver. comes with: 2B's tactical support unit - Pod 042, as well as the beautiful pure white sword - Virtuous Treaty, effect parts, an exchangeable body part to replicate 2B's self-destruct mode, and two additional headpieces that can be switched to evoke specific scenes.
This Deluxe package is a must-have for NieR: Automata fans as it enables them to replicate any scene from the game.
